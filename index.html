FAQ

Common questions

GuthrieAI is an AI-powered preconstruction support platform. We combine advanced Virtual Bid Assistants (VBAs) with a dedicated human Bid Coordinator to standardize, centralize, and automate your entire estimating workflow. Instead of replacing estimators, we eliminate administrative "grunt work" like parsing RFPs, tracking addenda, and managing RFIs so your team can focus on scope reviews, pricing strategy, and client relationships.

Every account is powered by a hybrid operational layer: 1 Virtual Bid Assistant (our proprietary AI workflow automation system) and 1 dedicated human Bid Coordinator. The AI handles instant document organization, tracking, and proposal generation templates, while your Bid Coordinator ensures operational visibility, monitors critical deliverables, and guarantees 100% compliance with RFI and bid deadlines.

The platform converts fragmented, email-heavy processes into structured data. For RFIs, GuthrieAI automatically tracks submission dates, logs open questions, and manages continuity across addenda. For proposals, our VBAs extract core project requirements, isolate trade-specific scope, map exclusions, and rapidly assemble compliant, professional responses so you can submit accurate bids much faster.
